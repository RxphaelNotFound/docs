LUCI is an AI companion designed to meet you where you are — whether you need help thinking through a problem, someone to talk to after a long day, or a thoughtful partner for creative projects. This guide walks you through everything from downloading the app to sending your very first message, so you can experience what a calm, human-centered AI conversation feels like in just a few minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download LUCI">
    LUCI is available on every major platform so you can pick up the conversation wherever you are.

    - **iOS** — Search for *LUCI: AI Companion* in the App Store and tap **Get**.
    - **Android** — Find *LUCI: AI Companion* on Google Play and tap **Install**.
    - **Web** — Open your browser and navigate to [luci.app](https://luci.app). No installation needed — LUCI runs entirely in your browser.

    Once the app opens (or the web page loads), you'll land on the welcome screen, ready for the next step.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Setting up your account takes less than a minute. On the welcome screen, tap **Get Started** and choose how you'd like to sign up:

    - **Email** — Enter your email address and create a password. LUCI will send a quick verification email; click the link inside to confirm your address and unlock full access.
    - **Continue with Google** — Authorize LUCI with your Google account in one tap.
    - **Continue with Apple** — Use Sign in with Apple to create your account privately.

    After authenticating, you'll be prompted to set a **display name** — the name LUCI will use when talking with you. You can use your real name, a nickname, or anything you like. You can always change it later in your profile settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your Initial Preferences">
    LUCI asks you three short questions to personalize the experience from the start. There are no right or wrong answers — just pick whatever feels most true to you:

    1. **How do you like to communicate?** Choose from *Concise and direct*, *Warm and conversational*, or *Detailed and thorough*.
    2. **What do you mainly want LUCI's help with?** Options include everyday planning, emotional support, learning new things, creative projects, or a little of everything.
    3. **How often do you want LUCI to check in?** Set a gentle rhythm — from *Only when I reach out* to *Daily nudges*.

    These preferences shape LUCI's tone and pacing right away. You can revisit and refine them any time from the [Preferences](/personalization/preferences) page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Say Hello">
    You're now on LUCI's main chat screen. At the top you'll see a soft greeting — something like *"Hey [your name], I'm glad you're here. What's on your mind?"* — and a calm, minimal interface designed to feel welcoming rather than overwhelming.

    To start your first conversation:

    - **Type a message** — Tap the input bar at the bottom of the screen, type whatever's on your mind, and press the **Send** arrow (or hit Enter on the web).
    - **Speak instead** — Tap the **microphone icon** in the input bar to use voice input. LUCI will transcribe your words and respond in kind.

    Don't worry about phrasing things perfectly. LUCI is designed to understand natural, everyday language — just talk (or type) the way you normally would.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  LUCI works offline too. Even without an internet connection, you can jot notes and scroll through your saved conversations. Your new messages will sync automatically the next time you're online.
</Tip>
