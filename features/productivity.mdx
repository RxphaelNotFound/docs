LUCI isn't just a conversation — it's a fully integrated system for managing what needs to get done. Alongside everything you can talk through with LUCI, you have a complete productivity layer built right in: tasks you can create in seconds, reminders that appear exactly when you need them, flexible scheduling tools, and focus modes that help you protect your time and energy. Everything works together, so the things you mention in conversation can become tasks, and the tasks on your list can become part of a plan LUCI helps you build.

## Tasks

You can create tasks in LUCI the moment you think of them — no friction, no switching apps.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Via Conversation">
    Just tell LUCI what you need to remember or get done. Natural language works perfectly:

    - *"Remind me to call the dentist tomorrow afternoon."*
    - *"Add 'review the contract' to my tasks with high priority."*
    - *"I need to send a thank-you note to Jordan by Friday."*

    LUCI will confirm the task it created, including any due date or priority it picked up from your message. If something isn't right, just say so — *"Actually, make that Thursday"* — and LUCI will update it on the spot.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Via Tasks Tab">
    Open the **Tasks** tab from the bottom navigation bar and tap the **+** button to create a task manually. You can fill in:

    - **Title** — what needs to be done
    - **Due date** — a specific date, time, or both
    - **Priority** — Low, Medium, or High
    - **Notes** — any additional context or details

    Tap **Save** to add the task to your list. You can edit any field at any time by tapping the task.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

When a task is done, swipe it right or tap the circle beside it to mark it complete. Completed tasks move to your **Archive**, where they stay visible for 30 days before being cleared. To access archived tasks, go to **Tasks → Archive** in the menu.

### Creating Your First Task via Conversation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a conversation with LUCI">
    Tap the chat icon from anywhere in the app to start or continue a conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your task in plain language">
    Tell LUCI what you need to do and when — for example, *"I need to submit the quarterly report by next Monday."* Include as much or as little detail as you like.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the task LUCI creates">
    LUCI will respond with a confirmation showing the task title, due date, and priority it inferred. Read it over to make sure everything looks right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm or adjust">
    Say *"Looks good"* to save the task as-is, or tell LUCI what to change — *"Set the priority to high"* or *"Move that to Wednesday instead."*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find it in your Tasks tab">
    Your new task now appears in the **Tasks** tab, sorted by due date. You can tap it at any time to add notes or make changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Tasks sync automatically across all your devices. Whether you create a task on your phone, tablet, or desktop, it appears everywhere within seconds. Make sure you're signed in to the same LUCI account on each device.
</Note>

## Reminders

LUCI's reminders go beyond simple alarms — they're designed to reach you at the right moment, in the right way.

**Time-based reminders** fire at a specific date and time. You can set them conversationally (*"Remind me at 3pm to take a break"*) or through the task and reminder editors. You choose whether reminders appear as notifications, play a sound, or both — adjust these preferences in **Settings → Notifications**.

**Location-based reminders** trigger when you arrive at or leave a place. Say something like *"Remind me to grab milk when I'm near the grocery store"* and LUCI will ask you to confirm the location, then watch for it. Location reminders require location permissions to be enabled — LUCI will prompt you if they aren't.

**Smart reminders** are suggestions LUCI makes based on your conversations. If you mention you have a call with someone next week, LUCI might quietly suggest a reminder to prepare beforehand. These suggestions appear as gentle prompts — you're never automatically opted in. Tap **Set reminder** on any suggestion to add it, or dismiss it with no friction.

## Schedules & Planning

LUCI gives you two views to stay oriented across time:

- **Daily view** — a chronological list of today's tasks, reminders, and any scheduled events, organized by time of day.
- **Weekly overview** — a broader view showing the shape of your week: where your commitments cluster, where you have breathing room, and what's coming up that deserves attention.

To generate a suggested day plan, just ask: *"Can you help me plan my day?"* LUCI looks at your tasks, their priorities and due dates, the time you have available, and any patterns it's learned about when you do your best work. It then offers a structured schedule — a time-blocked plan that puts the right things at the right times.

You're always in the driver's seat with these suggestions:
- **Accept** the full plan with one tap
- **Adjust** individual time blocks by tapping and editing them
- **Reject** the plan entirely and ask LUCI to try again with different constraints (*"I'd prefer to do deep work in the morning"*)

LUCI's scheduling suggestions improve over time as it learns what kinds of plans work for you and which ones you tend to move around.

## Focus Modes

Focus modes let you shape the environment LUCI creates around you — quieting distractions when you need to concentrate, softening the experience when you're winding down, and grounding you in what matters most at the start of your day.

**Deep Work** — Designed for stretches of sustained concentration. Notifications are minimized to only the most urgent. LUCI won't proactively suggest new tasks or send check-ins. The interface shifts to a quieter visual state. Activate it by tapping **Focus → Deep Work** or saying *"Start Deep Work mode."*

**Wind Down** — A gentler mode for the end of your day or any moment when you need to decompress. Reminders become softer in tone. LUCI's responses carry a calmer, more unhurried quality. New tasks can still be created, but LUCI won't push you to act on them. Activate it from **Focus → Wind Down** or by asking LUCI to switch modes.

**Morning Flow** — Start your day with intention. When Morning Flow is active, LUCI opens with a brief overview of your day: your top priorities, any time-sensitive reminders, and a light check-in about how you're feeling. It's designed to ease you in rather than throw the full weight of your schedule at you at once. Activate it from **Focus → Morning Flow** or set it to turn on automatically at a time you choose in **Settings → Focus**.

To deactivate any focus mode, tap **Focus → Off** or tell LUCI *"End focus mode."*
