LUCI's conversational AI is built around one idea: that a truly helpful companion meets you where you are. Whether you're working through a complicated problem, looking for a quick answer, or just need someone to think out loud with, LUCI responds with calm, considered language — never rushed, never robotic. Over time, LUCI adapts to the way you communicate, so every conversation feels less like querying a tool and more like talking to someone who actually knows you.

## Natural Dialogue

LUCI doesn't communicate in bullet points and boilerplate — it speaks to you in clear, warm prose that feels like a real exchange. When your message is ambiguous, LUCI asks a focused clarifying question rather than guessing or overwhelming you with assumptions. And over time, LUCI pays attention: to the vocabulary you use, the level of detail you prefer, and whether you like responses that are brief and direct or expansive and exploratory.

This adaptability means LUCI gradually matches your energy. If you tend to be concise, LUCI learns to be concise. If you enjoy a bit of warmth and humor in a conversation, LUCI brings that too. The result is a conversational rhythm that feels natural rather than transactional.

## Context & Memory

A conversation with LUCI is more than a single exchange — it builds. LUCI maintains context at two levels so that interactions always feel coherent and connected.

**Session context** means LUCI remembers everything you've discussed within your current conversation. You can refer back to something you mentioned twenty messages ago and LUCI will know exactly what you mean. You never have to re-explain yourself mid-conversation.

**Long-term memory** means LUCI carries certain things forward between sessions — your preferences, recurring topics, things you've mentioned are important to you, and the communication style you've settled into together. This is what allows LUCI to feel like it knows you rather than starting from scratch every time you open the app.

You're always in control of what LUCI remembers. To review, edit, or clear your memory at any time, go to **Settings → Privacy → Memory**. You can delete individual memories or wipe everything — whatever gives you the level of comfort you need.

## Conversation Tips

Getting the most out of LUCI is mostly about communicating naturally, but a few habits go a long way:

- **Be specific about what you want.** "Help me think through a difficult conversation with a coworker" gives LUCI much more to work with than "I need advice." The more context you share, the more useful the response.
- **Build on what came before.** You don't need to repeat yourself — refer to earlier parts of the conversation freely. LUCI is following along, and a message like "Can you expand on that second option?" works perfectly.
- **Use your own words.** You don't need to phrase things in any particular way or use technical language. LUCI is designed to understand natural, everyday speech — just talk the way you normally would.
- **Give feedback as you go.** If a response really lands, or misses the mark, let LUCI know. A quick thumbs up or thumbs down shapes how LUCI responds to you going forward.

<Tip>
  The feedback buttons (👍 / 👎) beneath each response aren't just for rating — they actively help LUCI recalibrate its tone, length, and approach for your future conversations. The more you use them, the better LUCI gets at responding in ways that feel right to you.
</Tip>

<Note>
  LUCI is designed to be a supportive, grounding presence. If a conversation moves toward content that could be harmful — to you or others — LUCI will gently acknowledge what you're sharing and guide things in a more constructive direction. This isn't about avoiding hard topics; LUCI can hold space for difficult emotions and complex situations. It's about making sure every conversation ends in a place that's safe and supportive.
</Note>
