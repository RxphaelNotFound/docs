Voice interaction is an optional layer that transforms LUCI from something you type to into something that genuinely feels like a companion. When you speak, LUCI listens — not in the stiff, command-driven way of traditional voice assistants, but with the same warmth and attentiveness it brings to text conversation. You can ask questions, talk through your day, set reminders, or just think out loud, all without touching your phone. For moments when your hands are busy, your eyes are elsewhere, or you simply want a more human-feeling exchange, voice mode is there.

## Enabling Voice Mode

Getting voice up and running takes just a moment.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings and navigate to Voice">
    From the main menu, tap **Settings**, then select **Voice**. This is where all voice preferences live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle Voice Mode on">
    Tap the toggle next to **Enable Voice Mode**. If this is your first time enabling it, your device will prompt you to grant LUCI microphone access — tap **Allow**. LUCI only uses the microphone when voice mode is active.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your activation style">
    Select how you want to start voice interactions:
    - **Tap to speak** — press and hold the microphone button whenever you want LUCI to listen. Great for occasional use or shared environments.
    - **Wake word ("Hey LUCI")** — speak the wake word at any time to start a voice session hands-free. LUCI listens passively for the wake word only, and begins processing after it hears it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose LUCI's voice and settings">
    Still in **Settings → Voice**, select your preferred voice style, and adjust speaking rate and pitch to your liking. You can preview any combination before saving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start your first voice session">
    Return to the home screen. If you chose tap-to-speak, press and hold the microphone icon. If you chose wake word, simply say *"Hey LUCI"* — and start talking.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Voice interaction requires an active internet connection for real-time speech processing. Offline voice support is in development and will be available in a future update. If you lose connectivity mid-session, LUCI will let you know and switch seamlessly to text mode.
</Note>

## Speaking with LUCI

Once voice mode is active, talking to LUCI works just like a natural conversation. You don't need to use special commands or a particular phrasing — speak the way you normally would.

When LUCI is listening, you'll see a **subtle waveform animation** on screen: a gentle, breathing pulse that responds to your voice in real time. It's designed to be calm and reassuring rather than urgent — a quiet visual confirmation that LUCI is with you. When LUCI is processing your message, the waveform shifts to a soft steady glow, and when it's ready to respond, you'll hear its voice and see the response appear as text at the same time.

A few things to know as you get comfortable with voice:

- **Speak at your natural pace.** LUCI doesn't require slow, deliberate speech. Normal conversational speed works perfectly.
- **Pause naturally.** Short pauses within a thought are fine — LUCI waits for a longer pause before treating your message as complete.
- **To end a voice session**, say *"Goodbye"* or *"That's all for now"*, or tap the **X** button that appears while the session is active. LUCI will close the voice session and return you to the standard interface.

If LUCI mishears something, just correct it in the moment — *"Actually, I said Thursday, not Tuesday"* — and LUCI will update its understanding without making it a big deal.

## LUCI's Voice

LUCI's text-to-speech is designed around one principle: comfort, not urgency. Its voice is calm, measured, and warm — a pace that doesn't rush you and a tone that doesn't feel pressured. It's meant to fit naturally into quiet moments, not compete with the noise of a busy day.

You can personalize LUCI's voice to suit you:

- **Speaking rate** — adjust how quickly LUCI speaks, from a gentle, unhurried pace to something crisper for when you want information faster.
- **Pitch** — shift LUCI's voice slightly lower or higher to find the register that feels most comfortable to you.

Both settings live in **Settings → Voice → Voice Preferences**. Changes take effect immediately — you'll hear a short preview as you adjust each slider.

LUCI's voice is not designed to sound urgent, insistent, or alarm-like. Even when delivering a time-sensitive reminder, the delivery stays measured. The goal is always a companion that supports your calm, not one that adds to your stress.

## Voice Shortcuts

You can speak these commands at any point during a voice session to quickly complete common tasks. LUCI understands natural variations, so you don't need to say them word for word.

| Command | What It Does |
|---|---|
| *"Set a reminder for [time/task]"* | Creates a time-based or smart reminder and confirms the details with you |
| *"Add a task: [description]"* | Creates a new task, including any due date or priority you mention |
| *"Read my day"* | Gives you a spoken summary of today's tasks, reminders, and scheduled events |
| *"Switch to Focus Mode"* | Activates your most recently used focus mode, or asks which one you'd like |
| *"Start Deep Work mode"* | Activates Deep Work focus mode directly |
| *"Start Wind Down mode"* | Activates Wind Down focus mode directly |
| *"Start Morning Flow"* | Activates Morning Flow focus mode directly |
| *"Start Focus Sanctuary"* | Activates Focus Sanctuary mode, removing all non-essential UI |
| *"End focus mode"* | Deactivates whichever focus mode is currently active |
| *"What did I ask you earlier?"* | LUCI summarizes the key points from earlier in your current voice session |
| *"Save this as a note"* | Saves the current conversation as a Note you can return to later |

## Privacy in Voice Mode

Your privacy in voice mode is straightforward, and LUCI is designed to be transparent about it.

**The microphone is only active when voice mode is on.** With tap-to-speak, it activates only while you're holding the button. With wake word mode, LUCI listens locally for the wake phrase only — this processing happens on your device and no audio is sent to LUCI's servers until the wake word is detected.

**No audio recordings are stored after processing.** When you speak, your audio is transcribed in real time and the transcript is used to generate LUCI's response. The raw audio is discarded immediately after transcription — it is never retained, analyzed, or associated with your account.

**You can review and clear your voice interaction history at any time.** Go to **Settings → Privacy → Voice History** to see a log of your transcribed voice sessions and delete any or all of it. Deleting your voice history removes it from your account and from LUCI's servers within 24 hours.

If you ever want to step back from voice mode entirely, you can disable it in **Settings → Voice** at any time. All your previous voice history can be deleted independently — disabling voice mode doesn't automatically clear it, so you can review it first if you'd like.
