While LUCI learns your habits over time on its own, Preferences is where you take the wheel. Think of it as your explicit instruction set — the settings you dial in yourself, rather than waiting for LUCI to infer. Anything you configure here takes effect immediately and stays exactly as you set it, giving you a reliable foundation beneath all of LUCI's adaptive behavior. Whether you want a darker interface, fewer notifications, or a more professional conversational tone, Preferences is where you make it yours from the start.

<Note>
Preferences and Adaptive Learning work as a team. Whatever you set here acts as your explicit instruction and always takes priority over anything LUCI has inferred on its own. Think of Preferences as the guardrails, and Adaptive Learning as the fine-tuning that happens within them.
</Note>

## Appearance

Shape how LUCI looks and feels on your screen. Find these settings at **Settings → Preferences → Appearance**.

| Setting | Options |
|---|---|
| **Theme** | Light, Dark, Auto (follows your device's system setting) |
| **Accent Color** | Choose from LUCI's curated palette of 12 colors — soft and purposefully calming |
| **Font Size** | Small, Medium (default), Large, Extra Large |
| **Ambient Animations** | On / Off — controls the soft background motion effects throughout the app |

Ambient animations add a gentle sense of life to LUCI's interface, but if you prefer a completely still environment — for focus or accessibility reasons — turning them off has no impact on functionality.

## Conversation Style

Tell LUCI exactly how you'd like it to communicate with you. These settings are available at **Settings → Preferences → Conversation Style**.

| Setting | Options |
|---|---|
| **Response Length** | Concise (brief, to the point), Balanced (default), Detailed (thorough explanations) |
| **Tone** | Warm & Casual, Neutral, Professional |
| **Language** | Choose from all supported languages and regional locales |

- **Concise** works well if you're moving fast and want quick answers.
- **Balanced** is LUCI's default — informative without being overwhelming.
- **Detailed** is ideal when you want LUCI to think out loud, explain its reasoning, or give you the full picture.

Your tone preference shapes everything from how LUCI opens a conversation to how it phrases suggestions. Warm & Casual feels like chatting with a friend; Professional keeps things crisp and focused.

## Notifications

Stay informed without feeling pestered. LUCI's notification system is designed to be unobtrusive by default, and fully customizable at **Settings → Preferences → Notifications**.

**Per-feature toggles:**

| Notification Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Task Reminders** | Alerts for tasks you've created or asked LUCI to track |
| **Daily Check-in** | A gentle morning prompt to review your day ahead |
| **Focus Mode Alerts** | Reminders when a scheduled Focus Sanctuary session is about to begin |
| **New Features** | Occasional heads-up when LUCI introduces something new |

**Quiet Hours:**
Set a time range during which LUCI won't push any notifications — no matter how urgent they might otherwise be. Navigate to **Notifications → Quiet Hours**, choose your start and end times, and select which days it applies. This is separate from Focus Sanctuary and applies passively at all times.

## Haptics & Animations

For mobile users, LUCI supports a range of tactile and motion settings to match your physical preferences. Find these at **Settings → Preferences → Haptics & Animations**.

| Setting | Options |
|---|---|
| **Haptic Feedback** | On / Off — subtle vibrations on interactions (mobile only) |
| **Reduce Motion** | On / Off — disables parallax effects and animated transitions |
| **Animation Speed** | Slow, Normal (default), Fast — controls the pace of UI transitions |

If you use LUCI in environments where tactile feedback would be disruptive, or if motion effects cause discomfort, these controls let you tune the experience precisely.

## Accessibility

LUCI is built to be welcoming to everyone. Accessibility settings can be found at **Settings → Preferences → Accessibility**.

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| **Screen Reader Support** | Optimizes LUCI's interface for VoiceOver (iOS) and TalkBack (Android) |
| **High Contrast Mode** | Increases contrast across the interface for improved legibility |
| **Large Text** | Applies a larger base text size throughout the app |
| **Reduced Motion** | Removes non-essential animations and transitions |

These settings can also be inherited automatically from your device's system accessibility settings — LUCI checks for those on first launch and applies them as defaults. You can always override them here.
