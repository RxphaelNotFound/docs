In a world that constantly competes for your attention, Focus Sanctuary is LUCI's answer to the noise. It's the app's most intentional feature — a mode that quietly removes everything you don't need and leaves only what you're actually here for. No navigation clutter, no notification badges, no gentle nudges pulling you sideways. Just you, the task or thought in front of you, and a digital space that feels, genuinely, like a deep breath. Whether you're heading into two hours of focused writing, winding down at the end of a long day, or easing into a new morning, Focus Sanctuary creates the conditions for presence.

## What Focus Sanctuary Does

When you activate Focus Sanctuary, LUCI clears the stage. Here's what disappears:

- **Navigation bar** — the bottom tab bar and any sidebar navigation are hidden from view
- **Notification badges** — all unread counts and alert indicators are suppressed
- **Suggested prompts** — the quick-action chips and contextual suggestions are tucked away
- **Ambient animations** — all background motion effects are paused
- **Secondary sidebar items** — shortcuts, recent conversations, and auxiliary panels are hidden

What stays is exactly what you need:

- Your **current conversation or note**, front and center
- The **minimal input bar** — clean, distraction-free, ready when you are
- A small, unobtrusive **exit button** to leave whenever you're ready

Nothing is deleted or lost. The moment you exit Sanctuary, everything returns to exactly where you left it.

## Activating Focus Sanctuary

You can enter Focus Sanctuary in whichever way feels most natural to you:

1. **Tap the Sanctuary icon** — Look for the leaf-and-circle icon in your toolbar (top-right on desktop, bottom bar on mobile). One tap takes you in.
2. **Use your voice** — Say **"Hey LUCI, focus mode"** and LUCI will transition into Sanctuary immediately, no screen interaction required.
3. **Set a schedule** — In **Settings → Focus Sanctuary → Schedule**, you can configure recurring Sanctuary windows — for example, every weekday from 9:00am to 11:00am. LUCI will enter Sanctuary automatically and gently alert you a minute before it begins.

<Tip>
Pair Focus Sanctuary with voice interaction for a completely screen-free session. Once you're in Sanctuary, you can speak your thoughts, ask questions, and dictate tasks without ever touching your device — ideal for deep work environments where you want your hands completely free.
</Tip>

## Sanctuary Modes

Focus Sanctuary isn't one-size-fits-all. When you activate it, you can choose from three distinct modes — each shaped around a different kind of mental state.

  <Card title="Deep Work" icon="brain">
    Silence all notifications, shift to a dark background, and switch the interface to a monochrome palette. Everything unnecessary disappears. Designed for the kind of focus that requires you to completely disappear from the world for a while.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wind Down" icon="moon">
    Soft warm tones wash over the interface. New task creation is disabled so you're not tempted to add to your list. LUCI offers a gentle nudge to close out anything still open and prepare for rest. Perfect for the end of a workday or the hour before bed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Morning Flow" icon="sun">

You can set a default Sanctuary Mode for your scheduled sessions, or choose one each time you activate it manually.

## Scheduling Sanctuary

Building a consistent rhythm is one of the most effective ways to protect your focus long-term. LUCI makes it easy to schedule recurring Sanctuary windows that activate automatically, no willpower required.

To set up a schedule, go to **Settings → Focus Sanctuary → Schedule**. You'll find a calendar-style picker where you can:

- **Select days of the week** — toggle individual days or apply a preset like "Weekdays" or "Every day"
- **Set a time window** — choose a start and end time using the time dial, with 15-minute granularity
- **Choose a default mode** — assign Deep Work, Wind Down, or Morning Flow to each scheduled window
- **Add multiple windows** — you can create separate entries for different parts of your day (e.g., a Morning Flow at 8am and a Deep Work block at 2pm)

LUCI will send a soft heads-up notification one minute before a scheduled Sanctuary session begins — just enough notice to wrap up a thought before it starts.

## Exiting Sanctuary

Leaving Focus Sanctuary is as easy as entering it:

- **Swipe down** from the top of the screen (mobile)
- **Tap the exit button** — the small icon that remains visible in the corner throughout your session
- **Use your voice** — say **"Hey LUCI, exit focus mode"** to step out hands-free

When you exit, LUCI will softly ask: *"Would you like to set a follow-up reminder?"* — giving you the option to schedule a check-in, continue a task later, or simply close out cleanly. You can dismiss this with a single tap if you don't need it.

Your notifications, navigation, and suggestions all return to exactly how they were before you entered. Nothing carries over from Sanctuary into your regular session unless you choose it.
