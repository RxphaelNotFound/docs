## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is LUCI and how is it different from other AI assistants?">
    LUCI is a calm, human-centered AI companion designed to support you — not overwhelm you. While many AI assistants are built around speed, task completion, and constant engagement, LUCI's philosophy is **presence without pressure**. That means LUCI meets you where you are: it listens, helps you think things through, and adapts to your pace rather than demanding your attention.

    LUCI isn't trying to replace a therapist, a productivity suite, or a search engine. It's a thoughtful space where you can reflect, write, plan, and simply feel heard — all in one place. The experience is intentionally gentle, distraction-free, and built around your wellbeing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is LUCI always available, or does it have downtime?">
    LUCI is designed to be there when you need it. We maintain high availability across our infrastructure and aim for 99.9% uptime. Occasionally, brief scheduled maintenance windows are required — when they are, we announce them in advance through the app and at [luci.app/status](https://luci.app/status).

    If you ever find LUCI unresponsive or slow, checking the status page is a great first step. You can also use LUCI's offline mode for journaling and saved content even when your connection is limited.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What devices and platforms does LUCI support?">
    You can use LUCI on:

    - **iOS** — iPhone and iPad running iOS 16 or later
    - **Android** — devices running Android 10 or later
    - **Web** — any modern browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge) at [luci.app](https://luci.app)

    Your conversations, notes, and preferences stay in sync across all your devices automatically, so you can pick up right where you left off — whether you switch from your phone to your laptop or the other way around.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is LUCI free to use?">
    Yes — LUCI has a free tier that gives you access to its core features, including conversations, journaling, basic reminders, and a limited conversation history. It's a full, meaningful experience at no cost.

    If you'd like to go deeper, **LUCI Plus** is our subscription plan. It unlocks:

    - **Advanced AI** — richer, more nuanced responses powered by our most capable models
    - **Unlimited conversation history** — access every conversation you've ever had with LUCI
    - **Extra customization** — personalize LUCI's tone, themes, and interaction style to feel truly your own

    You can upgrade to LUCI Plus anytime from **Settings → Account → LUCI Plus**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does LUCI share my conversations with anyone?">
    No. Your conversations with LUCI are private by default and are never sold, shared with advertisers, or used to build profiles for third parties. We use your conversations only to power your LUCI experience — for example, to provide context-aware responses and surface relevant memories you've shared.

    A small number of conversations may be reviewed by our team to improve LUCI's safety and quality, but this is done under strict privacy controls and your data is never linked to identifying information in those reviews. You can opt out of this in **Settings → Privacy → Improve LUCI**.

    You can read the full details in our [Privacy Policy](https://luci.app/privacy).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use LUCI completely offline?">
    LUCI supports a meaningful offline experience for certain features. When you don't have an internet connection, you can:

    - Read and write in your **journal**
    - Browse **saved notes and tasks**
    - Review your **recently synced conversation history**

    AI-powered conversations require an internet connection, since LUCI's responses are generated in real time. When you come back online, any journal entries or notes you created offline sync automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I delete all my data?">
    You're always in control of your data. To permanently delete everything associated with your account:

    1. Open **Settings → Account → Data & Privacy**
    2. Tap **Delete My Account and All Data**
    3. Confirm your decision when prompted

    This permanently removes your conversations, journal entries, notes, preferences, and account information from our servers within 30 days. This action cannot be undone, so please make sure you've exported anything you'd like to keep first — you can do that from the same menu by tapping **Export My Data**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my voice recorded and stored?">
    When you use voice mode, LUCI processes your speech to convert it to text so it can respond to you. This processing happens in real time and the raw audio is **not stored** on our servers after transcription. Only the text transcript of what you said is saved, just like any other conversation.

    You can review and delete your conversation history — including voice transcripts — at any time from **Settings → Account → Data & Privacy → Conversation History**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can LUCI set reminders from a conversation?">
    Yes — just ask naturally. You can say something like *"Remind me to call my doctor on Thursday at 10am"* or *"Set a reminder for my medication every day at 8pm"* and LUCI will create the reminder for you without any extra steps.

    Reminders appear in LUCI's **Reminders** tab and, if you've granted notification permission, as push notifications at the right time. You can also review, edit, or delete reminders directly in that tab whenever you like.

    Make sure notification permissions are enabled for LUCI in your device settings to receive timely alerts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What languages does LUCI support?">
    LUCI currently supports the following languages for conversation and journaling:

    - English
    - Spanish
    - French
    - German
    - Portuguese
    - Japanese
    - Simplified Chinese

    LUCI detects your language automatically based on how you write or speak to it, or you can set a preferred language in **Settings → General → Language**. We're actively expanding language support — more are on the way.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Focus Sanctuary work?">
    Focus Sanctuary is LUCI's dedicated space for deep work and intentional presence. When you enter Focus Sanctuary, LUCI dims distractions, silences non-urgent notifications, and creates a calm, minimal interface so you can concentrate fully on what matters.

    You can set a **Focus intention** — a short phrase describing what you're working on — and LUCI will gently hold you to it, offering check-ins at whatever interval feels right to you. You can also choose ambient sounds, set a session timer, and tell LUCI how you'd like to be supported (quietly in the background, or with occasional gentle prompts).

    To start a session, tap the **leaf icon** in the navigation bar or say *"Start Focus Sanctuary."*
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use LUCI for journaling or long-form writing?">
    Absolutely — journaling is one of LUCI's most loved features. The **Journal** tab gives you a clean, distraction-free writing space where your entries are private, automatically dated, and beautifully organized.

    If you're not sure where to start, LUCI can offer a gentle prompt — just tap **Inspire me** at the top of the Journal screen. You can also ask LUCI to help you expand a thought, reflect on a pattern across past entries, or simply listen as you write freely.

    For longer writing projects, LUCI supports rich text formatting, word count tracking, and the ability to organize entries with tags. Everything syncs across your devices so your writing is always with you.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account & Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade to LUCI Plus?">
    Upgrading takes just a moment. Here's how:

    1. Open **Settings → Account**
    2. Tap **Upgrade to LUCI Plus**
    3. Choose your preferred plan — monthly or annual (annual saves you over 30%)
    4. Complete the purchase through your App Store, Google Play, or payment method on the web

    Your LUCI Plus features activate immediately after your purchase is confirmed. If you don't see them right away, try signing out and back in, or visit **Settings → Account → Restore Purchases**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use LUCI on multiple devices?">
    Yes. One LUCI account works across all your devices simultaneously. Sign in with the same email and password on your iPhone, Android tablet, or the web, and everything — your conversations, journal, notes, reminders, and preferences — stays in sync automatically.

    LUCI Plus subscribers can have their account active on up to **five devices** at once. Free tier accounts support up to **two devices** at a time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
    You can cancel LUCI Plus at any time with no cancellation fee. Here's how, depending on where you subscribed:

    - **iOS (App Store):** Go to your iPhone's **Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → LUCI Plus → Cancel Subscription**
    - **Android (Google Play):** Open the **Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → LUCI → Cancel**
    - **Web:** Go to **luci.app → Settings → Account → Manage Subscription → Cancel**

    Your LUCI Plus access continues until the end of your current billing period, so you won't lose access immediately after canceling.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel?">
    Your data stays safe. If you cancel LUCI Plus, your account reverts to the free tier — you keep everything you've created (conversations, journal entries, notes, reminders), but access to Plus-exclusive features like unlimited history and advanced AI is paused.

    Your full conversation history remains stored and becomes accessible again the moment you resubscribe. We don't delete your data just because your subscription ends.

    If you choose to delete your account entirely, see *"How do I delete all my data?"* above for the steps.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
