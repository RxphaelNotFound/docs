LUCI (Live User Companionship Interface) is your personal AI companion, built around one philosophy: **presence without pressure**. Whether you need to plan your day, think through a problem, find creative inspiration, or simply have a calm conversation, LUCI is always available — never overwhelming.

This documentation covers everything you need to get the most out of LUCI: setting up your account, understanding its core features, personalizing your experience, and staying in control of your data.

## Designed for You

LUCI's interface is built around readability and emotional comfort — soft glassmorphism visuals, gentle transitions, and intuitive gestures that get out of your way. Everything from the typography to the ambient animations is tuned to reduce friction and increase calm.
