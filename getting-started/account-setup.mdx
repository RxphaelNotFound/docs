Your LUCI account is the foundation of your experience — it stores your preferences, conversation history, and personalization settings so everything is ready for you wherever you open the app. This page covers the full lifecycle of your account: from signing up for the first time, to customizing your profile, to securing your account and managing every device you use LUCI on.

<Note>
  LUCI never sells your personal data. Your profile information — including your display name, avatar, and bio — is private and visible only to you. It is never shared with third parties or used for advertising.
</Note>

## Creating Your Account

You can create a LUCI account using your email address or by linking an existing social login. Both paths take under a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open LUCI and tap Get Started">
    Launch the LUCI app on iOS, Android, or visit [luci.app](https://luci.app) in your browser. On the welcome screen, tap or click **Get Started** to open the signup flow.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your signup method">
    Select the option that works best for you:

    - **Email address** — Type your email and create a strong password (at least 8 characters). LUCI will send a verification email to confirm your address.
    - **Continue with Google** — Tap the Google button and authorize LUCI through your Google account. No separate password needed.
    - **Continue with Apple** — Use Sign in with Apple. You can choose to share or hide your real email address using Apple's private relay feature.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your email (email signup only)">
    If you signed up with an email address, check your inbox for a message from **hello@luci.app** with the subject line *Confirm your LUCI account*. Click the **Verify Email** button inside.

    If the email doesn't appear within a couple of minutes, check your spam folder or tap **Resend verification email** inside the app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your display name">
    Enter the name you'd like LUCI to call you — your real first name, a nickname, or anything that feels right. This name appears in LUCI's greetings and throughout the conversation interface. You can change it at any time from your profile settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete the preference questions">
    LUCI will ask three quick questions to personalize your experience right away. Answer honestly — these shape LUCI's communication style from your very first message. You can update your answers at any time in [Preferences](/personalization/preferences).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your Profile

Your profile holds the personal details that make your LUCI experience feel uniquely yours. To access it, tap your **avatar** in the top-right corner of the app, then select **Edit Profile**.

**Display Name**
Your display name is how LUCI addresses you in conversation — in greetings, check-ins, and personalized responses. Choose something you're comfortable hearing often. Changes take effect immediately.

**Avatar**
Your avatar appears on the profile screen and at the top of the chat interface. You can:
- Upload a photo from your device's camera roll.
- Take a new photo directly in the app.
- Choose one of LUCI's built-in illustrated avatars if you prefer not to use a personal photo.

**Bio**
The bio field is a short, optional note about yourself. LUCI reads your bio as a piece of context — for example, if your bio mentions that you're a teacher, LUCI may naturally tailor explanations and examples to that background. Keep it as brief or as detailed as you like.

## Security Settings

Keeping your account secure is straightforward. Navigate to **Settings → Security** to find the following options.

**Changing Your Password**
Tap **Change Password**, enter your current password to confirm your identity, then set a new one. LUCI will sign you out of all other active sessions after a password change as a precaution.

**Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**
Enable 2FA to add a second layer of protection to your account. LUCI supports authenticator apps (such as Google Authenticator or Authy) and SMS verification. Once enabled, you'll enter a one-time code in addition to your password whenever you sign in on a new device.

To turn it on:
1. Go to **Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication**.
2. Choose **Authenticator App** or **SMS**.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete setup.

**Active Sessions**
The **Active Sessions** panel shows every device currently signed in to your account — including the device type, operating system, approximate location, and last-seen time. If you see a session you don't recognize, tap **Sign Out** next to it immediately and consider changing your password.

## Managing Devices

LUCI is designed to follow you seamlessly across all of your devices. Your conversations, preferences, and saved notes stay in sync so you can start a thought on your phone and pick it up on the web without missing a beat.

**Signing In on Multiple Devices**
You can be signed in on as many devices as you like simultaneously. Simply download the app (or open luci.app) on another device and sign in with the same credentials. LUCI will sync your data automatically over Wi-Fi or cellular data.

**Sync Behavior**
- **When online**, all conversation history, preferences, and profile changes sync to the cloud in real time.
- **When offline**, LUCI saves your notes and messages locally. Everything syncs the next time your device connects to the internet — no manual action required.

**Signing Out Remotely**
If you've left yourself signed in on a device you no longer have with you — a borrowed computer, for example — you can sign that device out from anywhere:

1. Go to **Settings → Security → Active Sessions**.
2. Find the device you want to remove.
3. Tap **Sign Out** next to that session.

The device will be signed out within seconds. Any unsynced local data on that device will not be uploaded.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my email address?">
    Yes. Go to **Settings → Account → Email Address** and tap **Change Email**. Enter your new email address and confirm your password. LUCI will send a verification link to your new address — click it to complete the change. Until you verify the new address, your original email remains active. Note that if you signed up with Google or Apple, your email is managed by those providers; you'll need to update it there instead.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I forget my password?">
    On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password?** and enter your email address. LUCI will send a password-reset link that is valid for 30 minutes. Click the link, enter a new password, and you'll be signed back in automatically.

    If you no longer have access to the email address on your account, contact LUCI support at **support@luci.app** with proof of account ownership and the team will help you recover access.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
    You can permanently delete your LUCI account from **Settings → Account → Delete Account**. Before deletion, LUCI will ask you to confirm your password and will clearly explain that this action is **irreversible** — all your conversations, preferences, and profile data will be permanently erased and cannot be recovered.

    If you're having an issue that's making you consider leaving, LUCI support is happy to help at **support@luci.app** — sometimes a settings change or a quick conversation is all it takes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
